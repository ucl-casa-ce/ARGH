ARGH! Mateys

Augmented Reality Grounded Heritage: Portholes for Environmental and Youth Stories

Discover site-specific writing and spoken word poetry commissioned from local young people hidden in digital portholes located throughout the Royal Docks. Interactive web map is available at arghmateys.org

ARGH! Mateys has been created by researchers from the Connected Environments Lab together with young people from Youth Empowerment, Fight for Peace, West Silvertown Foundation and Royal Docks Learning Activity Centre.

ARGH! Mateys was commissioned by the Royal Docks for Join the Docks Autumn Festival 2020
